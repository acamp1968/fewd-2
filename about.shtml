About Us

Creative Property Resolutions was established to assist homeowner's who are experiencing distress through foreclosures, bankruptcies, estate sales and unsellable properties.

Creative Property Resolutions, LLC helps homeowners eliminate their liability and worry when faced with leaving their home. CPR also:

  • Provides the homeowner with funds to relocate or cover the costs of a bankrupcy filing
  • Prepares all of the transfer documents
  • Covers the costs of the homeowner's attorney at closing and all related filing fees

In return, CPR undertakes the homeownere's prior burden of protecting and preserving the property until it is sold or returned to the lender, which translates into safer neighborhoods and communities. CPR protects the homeowner's reputation by discreetly transferring the house to new ownership.
