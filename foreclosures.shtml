Foreclosures

We accept properties in foreclosure

The bank files a notice of default that states the owner has fallen behind on there mortgage payments or has defaulted on there mortgage loan.

Foreclosure:

Is the process where the homeowner transfers the right of ownership of the home to the bank or a lender after the owner defaults (falls behind on payments to their loan). Properties go into foreclosure if they usually miss 3 or more payments on there loan. Foreclosure can impact negatively on the homeowners credit score.

Post-Foreclosure:

The lender has taken control of the property or the property is in the hands of a new owner or investor who purchased the property at auction.
